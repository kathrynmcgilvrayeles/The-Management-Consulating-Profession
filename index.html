CMC-Global (ICMCI)

  • CMC-Global (ICMCI) is the international Management Consulting professional body.
  • CMC-Global has built a common standard for the individual certification of professional management consultants.
  • CMC designation is recognised around the world.
  • Learn more about the global consulting community

IMC Australia

  • IMC Australia is a founding member of ICMCI.
  • It holds a very prestigious position, having had numerous Presidents of ICMCI.
  • IMC Australia is a key part of the Asia-Pacific Hub.
  • IMC Australia maintains strong links with the global academic and business community, collaborating with NGOs like the UN and European Reconstruction Bank, and aligning with International Standards Organisation (ISO) standards.

Certified Management Consultant (CMC)

  • The Certified Management Consultant (CMC) is the mark of excellence in management consulting.
  • It signifies adherence to the CMC Competency Framework.
  • The CMC designation is a globally recognized standard for professional management consultants.
